Get to know Barefoot & Bundled

Hey, I’m Alex! Barefoot & Bundled was born from my love of beauty and fashion, but a habit of living in cozy sweat sets and athleisure. I’ve always dreamed of living in a beach town, which lends to my love for coastal-inspired pieces. I’m truly a coastal granddaughter at heart. Barefoot & Bundled is my mood board of inspiration and curated finds for a life that feels effortlessly dreamy, whether spent by the water or wrapped in soft layers at home. Here you’ll find my favorite beauty picks, everyday outfit ideas, and little luxuries that make life feel a bit sweeter.

Thanks for stopping by!
